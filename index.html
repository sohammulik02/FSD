About Our School

Kusumtai Thakare Bahuudhesh Sanstha Ghatanji

Established with a vision to provide comprehensive education and care for children with special needs, Kusumtai Thakare Bahuudhesh Sanstha Ghatanji stands as a beacon of hope and inclusion in the heart of Yavatmal, Maharashtra.

Our school is dedicated to nurturing the unique potential of each child, offering specialized programs that combine academic learning with therapeutic interventions. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their challenges.

With a team of compassionate educators, therapists, and support staff, we create a safe, loving environment where children can develop their skills, build confidence, and achieve their personal best. Our holistic approach encompasses intellectual development, emotional growth, and social integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school and admission process.

Children with intellectual disabilities, developmental delays, or special learning needs are eligible for admission. We welcome students of various ages and provide individualized education plans tailored to each child's unique abilities and requirements.

Our school offers specialized classrooms designed for inclusive learning, trained special education teachers, speech therapy, occupational therapy, and sensory integration facilities. We also provide vocational training, recreational activities, accessible infrastructure, and a safe, nurturing environment.

We are currently in the final stages of preparation and infrastructure development. The official opening date will be announced soon. Please follow our updates or contact us directly to receive notifications about our inauguration ceremony and admission schedule.
