A New Beginning: A School for Children with Special Needs in Yevatmal

By Admin | March 2026 | Yevatmal

A Place of Hope and Care

Located in Yevatmal, Kusumtai Thakare Bahuudesh Sanstha Ghatanji is preparing to open its doors as a dedicated school for children with special needs. Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing, and inclusive learning environment.

We believe that every child deserves love, respect, and the opportunity to grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

Building a Brighter Future

The school will provide specialized education programs, therapeutic support, and trained educators who understand the unique needs of each child.

Our goal is to empower every student with confidence, independence, and hope for a brighter tomorrow.
