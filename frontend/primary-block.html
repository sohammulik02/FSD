Primary stage

Primary Block

Grades 1 to 4 build the academic base. The stage mixes structured teaching with patient practice so children can strengthen reading, writing, and number work while gaining classroom independence.

What this stage supports

GoalSteady academic progress
MethodGuided practice and repetition
OutcomeConfidence with core subjects

What we focus on

The Primary Block moves children from readiness activities into clear academic routines. We keep the environment structured, warm, and practical so learning feels familiar and achievable.

Learning areas

  • Reading and comprehension practice
  • Writing, copying, and sentence building
  • Counting, basic operations, and number sense
  • General knowledge, computer awareness, and art

How we teach

Teachers use step-by-step instruction, visual aids, worksheets, and individual support. Every task is broken into small parts so students can work with confidence and finish stronger than they started.

Typical day

Warm-up revision, reading, or counting practice
Main lesson subject-based classroom learning
Practice time worksheets and guided activities
Wrap-up review, games, and confidence building

Skills we nurture

  • Reading simple sentences with support
  • Writing neatly and completing tasks
  • Following multi-step instructions
  • Working with a small group
