What we focus on

The Secondary Block strengthens academic learning while keeping a strong eye on everyday skills. Students are guided toward greater confidence, accountability, and self-expression.

Learning areas

  • Language, math, and general studies revision
  • Computer exposure and practical activities
  • Project work, oral practice, and reading support
  • Health, hygiene, and personal responsibility habits

How we teach

Lessons combine revision, discussion, hands-on tasks, and guided independence. We prepare learners for school completion by reinforcing confidence, consistency, and everyday decision-making.

Typical day

Review recap of previous learning
Main lesson core subject work and practice
Skills time computer, art, or life skills activity
Transition planning and reflection

Skills we nurture

  • Managing homework and classroom tasks
  • Reading for meaning and responding
  • Using practical skills in daily routines
  • Preparing for life beyond school
