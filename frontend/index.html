About Our School

Kusumtai Thakare Bahuudhesh Sanstha Ghatanji

Established with a vision to provide comprehensive education and care for children with special needs, Kusumtai Thakare Bahuudhesh Sanstha Ghatanji stands as a beacon of hope and inclusion in the heart of Yavatmal, Maharashtra.

Our school is dedicated to nurturing the unique potential of each child, offering specialized programs that combine academic learning with therapeutic interventions. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their challenges.

With a team of compassionate educators, therapists, and support staff, we create a safe, loving environment where children can develop their skills, build confidence, and achieve their personal best. Our holistic approach encompasses intellectual development, emotional growth, and social integration.

Student Life

Everyday growth through care, structure, and joy

Student life at our school blends academics, therapy, life skills, and co-curricular activities in a safe and encouraging environment. Each child is supported through routines that build confidence, communication, and independence.

Daily Routine

Morning welcome, classroom learning blocks, therapy slots, guided play, and calm closing activities.

Therapy and Support

Individual support through speech, occupational, physiotherapy, and behavioral care planning.

Life Skills

Self-care routines, communication, social behavior, and practical daily habits for independence.

Celebrations and Events

Festival celebrations, annual programs, sports activities, and theme days that build participation.

Achievements

Recognition of every step forward, from communication gains to classroom confidence and skill milestones.

Co-curricular Learning

Art, music, movement, sensory play, and classroom projects that improve expression and engagement.

Why choose us?

Our sole purpose

To be a leading institution of special education that honours the individuality of neurodiverse learners and equips them with the tools to live confident, independent, and purposeful lives. We envision a world where special education is not an alternative, but an empowering journey tailored to the unique abilities, needs, and pace of each student. With a foundation of structure, compassion, and expertise, we strive to redefine what success looks like for every neurodiverse child in our care.
